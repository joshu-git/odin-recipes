Strawberry Custard Pie

Ingredients For Custard Pie

  • 3 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • Unbaked 9-inch pie shell
  • 1 quart strawberries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Set oven for hot, 425Â°F.
  2. Combine eggs, sugar, and salt. Add milk slowly; stir in vanilla.
  3. Pour into pie shell. Bake 40 minutes, or till knife inserted near rim comes out clean.
  4. Wash strawberries;hull. Simmer 1 cup of the berries, sugar and 1 cup of water for fifteen minutes.
  5. Mix remaining water with cornstarch; add to cooked berries. Spoon over pie.
